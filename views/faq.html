What is Solaris?

Solaris is a decentralized open source cryptocurrency focused on privacy, innovation and the advancement of technology. Utilizing environmentally friendly Proof of Stake consensus protocol and a supporting secondary Masternode network, Solaris establishes itself as a secure, self-sustaining, decentralized and private cryptocurrency for everyday use.

How do I get Solaris/XLR?

Solaris, is actively traded on several cryptocurrency exchanges. For the most recent list of exchanges that trade Solaris, please click here.

Where can I use Solaris/XLR?

Solaris aims to be a widely used cryptocurrency via partnerships with online retailers and vendors, marketplace integrations and ease of use with instant transaction times. Solaris is integrated into the MyCryptoCheckout plugin, can be used to purchase goods and services on FLOGMall, and traded on various exchanges around the globe.

What is Txbit.io?

Txbit is proudly made and based in the Netherlands. A cryptocurrency exchange built from the ground up to meet today’s demands in security, privacy, reliability and speed. The proprietary trading engine and interface deliver a blazing-fast, world-class user experience catering to traders who enjoy spending some time behind the charts, but also to those who prefer to be in and out quickly.

Today’s cryptocurrency exchange industry is largely split into small, poorly managed and outdated exchanges, and the large, high volume exchanges that are near impossible to be listed on due to high fees and excessive bureaucracy. Txbit aims to fill this gap, providing a modern exchange platform for everyone to use. We are firm believers in growing the cryptocurrency industry without bankrupting legitimate projects in the process.

How is Solaris involved?

Solaris has part funded and participated in building the next generation cryptocurrency exchange txbit.io. Solaris XLR is a base market on txbit.io, together with BTC and ETH. All Solaris Mastenode operators and Stakers can also take part in the Solaris Reward Program.

Solaris Reward Program?

Solaris is the first cryptocurrency which enables a secondary layer of rewards for operating a masternode or actively staking XLR. All masternodes and staking address are eligible to receive 50% of the Net trading fees from txbit.io, after costs and expenses. Learn more at Solaris Reward Program.
